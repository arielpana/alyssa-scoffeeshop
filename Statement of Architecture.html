Statement of Architecture Work (SAW):

The primary goal of this architecture initiative is to align Alyssa's Coffee Shop technology and operational architecture with its vision of becoming the community’s go-to gathering place for exceptional coffee and delightful moments.

Key Goals Objectives:

  1. Customer-Centric Experience:
    Goal:

    Enhance the café’s architecture to create an exceptional customer experience.

    Objectives:

    Implement a state-of-the-art POS system for efficient order processing and payment handling.
    Develop a mobile app for convenient online ordering and reservations.
    Personalize customer experiences through a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems.

  2. Operational Efficiency:

    Goal:

    Improve operational efficiency through technology enhancements.
    Objectives:

    Implement inventory management solutions for efficient ingredient tracking and waste reduction.
    Streamline communication between front-of-house and kitchen staff with a Kitchen Display System (KDS).

  3. Community Engagement:

    Goal:

    Foster a sense of community and belonging. Objectives:

    Develop an online platform for customers to provide feedback and reviews.
    Host regular community events and collaborations to create a gathering place.
    Utilize social media to connect with the community and share café updates.

  4. Data Security and Compliance:

    Goal:

    Ensure the security of customer and business data.
    Objectives:

    Implement robust cybersecurity measures to protect data.
    Comply with relevant data protection regulations to safeguard customer privacy.

  5. Innovation and Adaptability:

    Goal:

    Cultivate a culture of innovation and adaptability.
    Objectives:

    Invest in emerging technologies to stay competitive and adaptable.
    Encourage staff to propose and implement innovative ideas to enhance operations.
    Maintain a flexible technology architecture that can evolve with changing business needs.

  6. Budget Management:

    Goal:

    Effectively allocate and manage resources for architectural improvements.
    Objectives:

    Allocate the budget to projects and initiatives based on priority and alignment with strategic goals.
    Monitor and track budget utilization for each project.
    Provide regular budget reports and updates to stakeholders.

Business Functions:

  1. Menu Management: Create, price, and update the menu offerings.

  2. Customer Service: Provide excellent service and handle inquiries.

  3. Food Preparation: Prepare and present food and beverages.

  4. Inventory Control: Monitor and manage ingredient supplies efficiently.

  5. Marketing: Promote the coffe through campaigns and events.

  6. Financial Management:Handle transactions and track finances.

  7. Technology Management: Maintain and secure technology systems.

  8. Staff Management: Train, schedule, and supervise employees.

  9. Community Engagement: Foster community connections and host events.

  10. Quality Assurance: Ensure food quality and gather customer feedback.
