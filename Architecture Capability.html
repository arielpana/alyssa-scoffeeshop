Architecture Vission Scope:

The primary goal of this Enterprise Architecture (EA)

Initiative is to systematically assess and enhance the organization’s existing Architecture to align with strategic objectives, address identified gaps, and define A robust roadmap for the future. Over a five- year horizon, this initiative will Leverage an annual budget allocation of 500,000 Philippine Pesos to drive Transformational changes within the organization .

  1. Strategic Alignment Assessment Component:
    This component focuses on assessing the organization’s current architecture in relation to its strategic objectives. It involves:
    Conducting a comprehensive review of the organization’s strategic goals and objectives.
    Evaluating the existing architecture to identify misalignments and gaps.
    Collaborating with stakeholders to prioritize strategic initiatives that need architectural.

  2. Architecture Enhancement Component:
    This component is dedicated to making improvements and enhancements to the organization’s architecture. It includes:
    Identifying specific areas where the architecture needs enhancement, such as technology, processes, or data.
    Developing and implementing architectural solutions to address identified gaps and meet strategic goals.
    Ensuring that enhancements are scalable, cost-effective, and aligned with the budget allocation of 500,000 Philippine Pesos per year.
    Continuously monitoring and adapting the architecture to accommodate changing business needs and technological advancements.

  3. Transformational Roadmap Component:
  • This component involves creating a robust transformational roadmap that guides the organization’s architectural evolution over the five-year horizon. It includes:

  • Defining clear milestones and deliverables for each year of the initiative. Allocating the annual budget of 500,000 Philippine Pesos to specific projects and initiatives within the roadmap.

  • Identifying key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics to measure the success and impact of architectural changes

  • Ensuring that the roadmap is flexible and adaptable, allowing for adjustments based on envolving strategic priorities in external factors.
