Overview

Mission:

Is to enrich daily moments with the perfect cup of coffee, brewed with passion and precision


Vission:

To be a community hub , fostering connections and conversation over the finest coffee blends.


Goals and Objectives:

Goals:

  1. Quality Coffee Excellence: Achieve recognition as a destination for premium coffee.
  2. Customer Engagement: Build a loyal customer base .
  3. Sustainability Practices: Become an environmentally conscious coffee shop.
  4. Community Involvement: Actively contribute to the local community.
  5. Innovation in Offerings: Stay a head with unique and seasonal Offerings.

Objectives:

  1. Online Presence: Increase our social media following by 25% in the next year through consistent and engaging online content.

  2. Menu Expansions: Add at least three new menu items that cater to dietary preferences such as vegan , gluten, -free ,or low -sodium options within the next six months.

  3. Sustainability Initiatives: Implement a recycling program and reduce single use plastics, aiming to achieve a 15% reduction in waste within the next year.

  4. Community Partnership: Forge partnership with local charities and organizations to support community projects and initiatives.

  5. Financial Planning: Develop a comprehensive financial plan for the next five years, aiming for a 15% increase in Profitability by the end of year three.
